Changing color of objectives

Red indicates no understanding, yellow indicating some, and green indicating full.

These colors are meant to help users keep track of their learning process.


Changing the color of a learning objective to the corresponding user's level of understanding.

            
                //Makes the learning objective yellow
                learn.changeColor(1)

                //Makes the learning objective green. If called again, will go back to red.
                learn.changeColor(1)

Users can directly interact with the objectives through their own clicks.
