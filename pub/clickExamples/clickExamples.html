Changing color of objectives

Recall: Red indicates no understanding of learning objective, yellow indicates some understanding and green indicates complete understanding.

These colors are meant to help users keep track of their learning process. They can interact with these learning objectives as they progress through the material, keeping in mind their level of understanding.


Changing the color of a learning objective to the corresponding user's level of understanding.

            
                //Makes the learning objective yellow indicating some understanding of learning objective.
                learn.changeColor(1, "yellow")

Users can directly change the color of the objectives through their own clicks.
