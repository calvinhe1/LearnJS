Adding a learning objective

Add learning objectives to a webpage in an aesthetic way and inform users about what they will learn.


Create a list container and add learning objectives to it.

            
                const learn = LearnJS()

                //add list container to hold learning objectives.
                learn.addList()
                
                //Add learning objectives to the list, passing in the difficulty level.
                learn.addObjective("easy")
                learn.addObjective("intermediate")
                learn.addObjective("hard")

Users can also add their own learning objectives by clicking the plus button in the bottom of the list container (Note: must submit the form to successfully add)
